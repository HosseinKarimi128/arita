TECHNIQUE: Slow Motion
Parameters:
- Frame Rate: 60-120fps
- Speed Reduction: 0.25x - 0.5x
Best Used For:
- Emotional moments
- Action sequences
- Natural phenomena
Example Prompts:
- "slow motion sequence of falling autumn leaves, golden hour lighting, cinematic quality"
- "dramatic slow-motion walk, rain falling, moody atmosphere, anamorphic lens"

TECHNIQUE: Hyperlapse
Parameters:
- Frame Interval: 0.5-2 seconds
- Camera Movement: Smooth tracking
Best Used For:
- Urban scenes
- Nature transformations
- Construction processes
Example Prompts:
- "hyperlapse through busy city streets, day to night transition, smooth camera movement"
- "architectural hyperlapse, modern buildings, dynamic perspective shifts"
